I am co-organizing and speaking at the workshop **"COMPASS: Complexity, Markets, Policy, and AI in Social Systems"**, held at **Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna** in **Pisa**.

### Role & Contributions
- **Organizing Committee Member**
- **Speaker in Panel 3:** *AI for Complexity and Economic Systems* (16:00 – 17:15)

### Highlights
- Keynotes by **Prabhani Kuruppumullage Don**, **Guido Germano**, and **Federica Casarosa**.
- Panels covering agent-based modeling, legal & ethical implications of AI, and economic complexity.
