On **Monday, May 18th, 2026**, I will be co-organizing and speaking at the workshop **"COMPASS: Complexity, Markets, Policy, and AI in Social Systems"**, held at **Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna** in **Pisa**.

The workshop explores the transformative role of Artificial Intelligence in understanding and modeling complex social systems, bringing together researchers across disciplines to discuss the intersection of complexity, markets, and policy.

As a member of the **Organizing Committee**, I will also speak in **Panel 3: AI for Complexity and Economic Systems** (16:00 – 17:15), alongside Lorenzo Emer, Giuseppe Squillace, and Anna Gallo.

### Event Details
- **Date & Time:** Monday, May 18th, 2026 · 09:30 – 17:45
- **Venue:** Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Piazza Martiri della Libertà 33, Pisa (Room 3) & Online
- **Program:** Keynote lectures by Prabhani Kuruppumullage Don, Guido Germano, and Federica Casarosa, followed by thematic panels on modeling, ethics, and economic complexity.
