Our paper, **"Statistical model checking of the Island Model: an established economic agent-based model of endogenous growth"**, has been accepted for publication and presentation at **MARS @ ETAPS 2026**.

Co-authored with **Giorgio Fagiolo**, **Daniele Giachini**, **Andrea Vandin**, and **Ernest Ivanaj**, the work demonstrates how **Statistical Model Checking** (SMC) via **MultiVeStA** enables automated, reproducible verification of agent-based macroeconomic models while preserving their underlying behavioral structure.

I presented the paper in **Turin, Italy, on Sunday, April 12, 2026**.
